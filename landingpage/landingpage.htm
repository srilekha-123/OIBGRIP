Know Yourself
Be Yourself

Knowing yourself is the beginning of all wisdom.To find yourself, think for yourself.Dont be confused between what people say you are and who you know you are. Socrates famously declared that the unexamined life was not worth living.Self-knowledge is important for one central reason: because it offers us a route to greater happiness and fulfilment.Armed with the right sort of self-knowledge, we have a greater chance of avoiding errors in our dealings with others and in the formulation of our life choices.
